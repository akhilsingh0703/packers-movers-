Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our services

How do you calculate moving costs?

Our pricing is based on several factors including distance, volume/weight of goods, packing materials needed, any special handling requirements (like pianos or artwork), and the services you select. We provide detailed, transparent quotes after assessing these requirements during our free consultation.

What items are prohibited from transport?

For safety and legal reasons, we cannot transport hazardous materials, firearms, ammunition, explosives, flammables, perishable food items, plants, pets, or high-value items like cash, jewelry, and negotiable securities. We'll provide a complete list when you book with us.

Can I pack my belongings myself?

Yes, you can pack your items yourself, though we recommend using our professional packing services for fragile and valuable items as our expertise ensures maximum protection during transit. Self-packed items will still be handled carefully but may not qualify for full insurance coverage.

How much advance notice do you need?

We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance, especially during peak moving seasons (summer months). However, we may be able to accommodate last-minute requests depending on availability. Emergency relocation services are available with priority surcharge.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, bank transfers, credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), digital wallets, and can generate GST invoices for corporate clients. A deposit is required at booking with the balance due before unloading at destination.
